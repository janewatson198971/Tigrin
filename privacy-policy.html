Clear summary: Tigrin does not collect any data. We do not store data and we do not share data with third parties.

1. Data collection

We do not collect personal information, sensitive data, device identifiers, or usage analytics. The app is designed to work without any data collection.

  • No account registration.
  • No analytics SDKs.
  • No advertising or tracking identifiers.
  • No background tracking.

2. Data storage

We do not store personal data on servers because we do not collect it. If the app stores basic settings locally on your device (for example, your preferences), this information remains on your device and is not transmitted to us.

3. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with anyone because we do not collect user data.

4. Third-party services

Tigrin does not use third-party services that collect user data (such as ads or analytics). If this changes in the future, we will update this policy before enabling any data collection.

5. Changes to this policy

If we update the app in a way that affects privacy, we will update this page. The policy is written to stay clear for both reviewers and users.
